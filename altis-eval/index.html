02

Four designs, four verdicts

Every design came back hackable-but-fixable — none infeasible. Pips show red-team soundness (attacker + auditor consensus, 10 = tight).

① human-label replay 5/10

Loop Materiality Replay

Replay production triage: frozen finding + thesis context + 14-day echo block → score 1/2/3, echo flag, calibrated confidence. Scored against held-out human votes.
R = .55·Sscore + .15·Secho + .15·Scal + .05·Sconsist + .10·Sconcise − Pmiss
Attack: predicting all-1s scores 0.788 against the 0.795 floor — a keyword net closes the gap. Holdout had 33 votes, not 300.
Fix: label-accrual campaign incl. suppressed findings, per-class recall floors, 3-class Brier, private rotated corner set.
⑤ live-api ground truth 5/10

Zoo-Router

The agent routes, it doesn't solve: task card + budget → model, effort, predicted quality & cost. Regret vs a hidden oracle sweep; hallucinated model ids zero the episode.
R = Gvalid × ( .65·Rroute + .20·Rlit + .15·Rcal )
Attack: 8 fixed budgets ≈ a 24-entry lookup table — RL memorizes it. Pricing isn't API-derivable; the repo's own table is 3 months stale.
Fix: continuous budgets, price perturbation with oracle recompute, tie-aware regret — and promote per-instance routing (1,288 free bandit instances) to primary.
③+④ agentic end-to-end 6/10

Day-0 Memo Gym

Frozen company snapshot at cutoff T, offline BM25 tools, ≤25 turns → cited first-look memo + 10 expert-call questions, scored against what analysts actually asked after T.
R = .35·Ccite + .30·Ccov + .25·Q + .10·H  · fabricated cite ⇒ R ≤ 0.2
Attack: hedged quote-stitching maxes citations and rides a loose 0.60 cosine into coverage; question gold is parroted from the snapshot itself.
Fix: n-gram extraction penalty, uncited prose counts as failure, novelty filter on question gold, company-level splits, judge injection suite.
② frozen-clock backtest 5/10

Almanac

(company, freeze date T, 30d) → probabilities over 8 material-event types, Code-Red incidence, and key-debate movement — resolved by the post-T monitoring stream.
R = .4·Revents + .3·Rcodered + .3·Rdebates  · gate: BSS > 0
Attack: rare events mean constant near-zero prediction earns ≈0.9; "always unresolved" harvests the debate term; article dates ≠ event dates (retrodiction).
Fix: train on skill-over-climatology, proper scoring on debates, snapshot mutable rows, extract event dates. Ship its by-product regardless: the Debate Resolution Ledger.

04

The sequence

  1. weeks 1–2
    Loop Materiality Replay

    Same function serves as training reward and production promotion gate — no train/deploy gap. Prereq: a 2–4 week label-accrual campaign plus a designed 3-rater agreement experiment.

  2. weeks 1–2 · same harness
    Zoo-Router oracle sweep

    Measure the cost/quality frontier for a job that runs 365×/year. The sweep is a procurement document by itself; its cached per-case predictions become free contextual-bandit instances.

  3. this quarter
    Day-0 Memo Gym

    The flagship agentic environment — the actual analyst job, fully offline and cacheable for cheap rollouts. Verifier doubles as production citation-QC from day one.

  4. side product
    Almanac → Debate Resolution Ledger

    Skip the RL claim for now; ship the ground-truth builder as a partner-facing "what changed since publication" artifact and a report-staleness alarm.

05

Reward-design checklist

Distilled from eight adversarial reviews. Apply before writing any reward code.

  • Constant-policy audit first. If a degenerate policy lands within noise of the gate, the reward is broken.
  • Skill-over-baseline inside the objective — not just on the leaderboard.
  • Count the minority class. With 93/5/2 imbalance the signal lives in 2% of cases.
  • Proper scoring rules only. Linear probability-mass rewards are one-hot-gameable.
  • Custody: the policy reads everything it can reach. Keys, sweeps, and gold live outside the sandbox.
  • Ask what the labels condition on. Votes only on alerted items = the miss side is invisible.
  • Event dates, not publication dates. Snapshot mutable rows; stride overlapping horizons.
  • Judges: pinned, hashed, cached, injection-tested — and calibrated on a task-matched anchor set.
  • Close the denominators. Fixed K, minimum claim counts, length caps.
  • Penalize extraction if you want synthesis — or RL converges on quote collage.
  • Version everything. Late labels mint new versions; never mutate scored rewards.
  • Every eval pays for itself with zero training — audit, sweep, QC harness, ledger.
